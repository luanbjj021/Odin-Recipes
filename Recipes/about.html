Recipe for a delicious Lasagna

Description

Who hasn't thought about surprising their family with a delicious lunch? Here's a wonderful recipe that will leave everyone speechless!

Ingredients

  • 500 g of Meat
  • 2 boxes of heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 3 tablespoons of flour
  • 500 g of ham
  • 500 g of Lasagna noodles
  • 500 g of mozzarella
  • salt to taste
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 grated onion
  • 3 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 box of tomato sauce
  • 3 cloves of garlic,crushed
  • 1 package of grated cheese

Steps

-lasagna

  1. Cook the noodles to the according manufacture's instructions,place them in a dish with cold water to prevent sticking,and set aside.

Bolognese sauce

  1. Sauce the gartic,onion,ground beef and tomato sauce. let it cook for 3 minutes,and set aside.

White sauce

  1. Melt the butter,add the 3 tablespoons of flour,and stir
  2. Gradually pour in the milk while continuing to stir
  3. Finally,add the heavy cream,stir for 1 minute and turn off the beat

Assembly

  1. Pour part of the Bolognese sauce into a baking dish add a half of the noodles,half of the ham,half of muzzarella,all of the white sauce,and the remaining noodles.
  2. Repeat the layers up to the top of the dish
  3. Finish with the grated cheese and bake in preaheated oven at high temperature (220C) for about 20 minutes
