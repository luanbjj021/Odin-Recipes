Simple Chocolate cake recipe

Description

A simple, rich chocolate cake everyone will love!

Ingredients

For the cake:
  • 1 ¾ cups flour
  • 1 ¾ cups sugar
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • ½ cup oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
For the Frosting:
  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • 3 ½ cups powdered sugar
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • ½ cup cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Steps

Make the batter:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease two 9-inch pans.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Mix until smooth.
  4. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin).

Bake the Cake:

  1. Divide batter into pans and bake for 30–35 minutes.
  2. Cool for 10 minutes, then transfer to a rack to cool fully.

Make Frosting:

  1. Beat butter until creamy. Add powdered sugar and cocoa gradually.
  2. Mix in cream and vanilla until fluffy.

Assemble:

  1. Frost the first cake layer, add the second, and cover with frosting.
